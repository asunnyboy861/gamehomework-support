Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a family?

Open the app and tap "Create a Family". Enter your family name, and you'll receive a unique 6-character code to share with family members.

How do family members join?

Family members can join by entering your family code in the "Join a Family" section. They'll need to download the app and create their own character first.

What's included in the free version?

The free version includes basic quests for 1 family member, limited rewards, and core gamification features.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are auto-renewable and billed through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel anytime in Settings > Subscriptions.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. This will sync your subscription across devices using the same Apple ID.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All data is stored securely in your iCloud account. We never sell or share your personal information.

Troubleshooting

iCloud sync not working? Make sure you're signed into iCloud with the same Apple ID on all devices.

Notifications not received? Check Settings > Notifications and ensure ChoreQuest has permission.

App crashing? Try force-closing the app and restarting. If issues persist, contact support.
